Biography

Namaskar, I am Dr Sivashanmugam from Chennai, practicing Ayurveda since 2010. I completed my BAMS Ayurveda from Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. I am registered with Indian association of Physiotherapist as a life member having completed my bachelor’s in Physiotherapy from Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. I work as a General practitioner and a herbal researcher with my focus on Neurological disorders, spine disorders and soft tissue injuries. The medicines I prescribe/provide to my patients are based on my research on the classical ayurvedic samhitas and literature.
